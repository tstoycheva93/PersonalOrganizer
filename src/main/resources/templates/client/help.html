ğŸš€ Getting Started

Welcome to Taskly! This guide will help you get started with our task management platform.

Creating Your First Task

  1. Navigate to the "My Tasks" page from the sidebar menu
  2. Click the "+" button in the top right corner
  3. Fill in the task details (title, description, due date, etc.)
  4. Click "Create Task" to save

Understanding the Dashboard

Your dashboard provides an overview of your tasks and productivity metrics. You can:

  • View upcoming deadlines
  • See your task completion rate
  • Check your productivity trends
  • Access your most recent tasks

ğŸ“ Task Management

Taskly offers powerful tools to help you manage your tasks efficiently.

Task Categories

Organize your tasks by categories to keep your workflow organized:

  • Work - Professional tasks and projects
  • Personal - Individual tasks and errands
  • Study - Educational and learning tasks
  • Health - Fitness and wellness activities
  • Shopping - Purchases and errands

Task Status

Track your progress with different task statuses:

  • Not Started - Tasks you haven't begun yet
  • In Progress - Tasks you're currently working on
  • Completed - Finished tasks
  • Archived - Tasks you want to keep but no longer need to track

Editing and Deleting Tasks

To edit a task, click on it to open the task details, then click the edit button. To delete a task, open the task details and click the delete button. You'll be asked to confirm before the task is permanently removed.

ğŸ“… Calendar Features

The calendar view helps you visualize your schedule and plan your time effectively.

Navigating the Calendar

Use the arrow buttons to move between months. The current month is displayed at the top of the calendar.

Viewing Tasks on the Calendar

Tasks with due dates will appear on the calendar. The number of tasks for each day is displayed in the calendar cell. Click on a day to see the detailed list of tasks for that day.

Adding Tasks from the Calendar

You can add a new task directly from the calendar view by clicking on a day and then the "+" button in the task list section.

Task Completion Rate

The task completion rate shows the percentage of tasks you've completed within a given time period. This helps you assess your productivity and set realistic goals.

Task Distribution

The task distribution chart shows how your tasks are distributed across different categories. This helps you balance your workload and identify areas that might need more attention.

Time Management

The time management chart shows when you're most productive during the day. Use this information to schedule important tasks during your peak productivity hours.
