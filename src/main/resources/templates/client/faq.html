üë§ Account & Settings

How do I change my password?

To change your password:

  1. Go to the Account page
  2. Click the "Edit" button in the bottom right corner
  3. Click "Change password"
  4. Enter your current password and your new password
  5. Click "Update Password"
Can I change my profile photo?

Yes, you can change your profile photo by:

  1. Going to the Account page
  2. Clicking the "Edit" button in the bottom right corner
  3. Hovering over your profile photo and clicking "Change Photo"
  4. Selecting a new image from your device
  5. Confirming the change

How do I delete my account?

To delete your account:

  1. Go to the Account page
  2. Click the "Edit" button in the bottom right corner
  3. Scroll down and click "Delete account"
  4. Confirm your decision in the popup dialog

Warning: This action cannot be undone. All your data will be permanently deleted.

üìù Tasks & Projects

How do I create a new task?

There are several ways to create a new task:

  1. From the My Tasks page: Click the "+" button in the top right corner
  2. From the Calendar page: Click on a day and then the "+" button in the task list
  3. From any page: Use the keyboard shortcut Ctrl+N (Windows) or Cmd+N (Mac)

Fill in the task details and click "Create Task" to save.

How do I edit or delete a task?

To edit a task:

  1. Click on the task to open its details
  2. Click the "Edit" button
  3. Make your changes
  4. Click "Save Changes"

To delete a task:

  1. Click on the task to open its details
  2. Click the "Delete" button
  3. Confirm the deletion in the popup dialog
How do I mark a task as completed?

There are two ways to mark a task as completed:

  1. From the task list: Click the checkbox next to the task
  2. From the task details: Open the task and click the "Mark as Completed" button

Completed tasks will be moved to the "Completed" section of your task list.

Can I set reminders for tasks?

Yes, you can set reminders for tasks with due dates:

  1. Create or edit a task
  2. Set a due date for the task
  3. Enable the "Reminder" option
  4. Select when you want to be reminded (e.g., 1 day before, 1 hour before)

You'll receive notifications at the specified time.

üìÖ Calendar & Scheduling

How do I view my tasks on the calendar?

Tasks with due dates automatically appear on the calendar. The number of tasks for each day is displayed in the calendar cell. Click on a day to see the detailed list of tasks for that day.

Can I change the calendar view?

Currently, Taskly only offers a monthly calendar view. We're working on adding weekly and daily views in a future update.

How do I add a task directly from the calendar?

To add a task directly from the calendar:

  1. Click on a day in the calendar
  2. Click the "+" button in the task list section
  3. Fill in the task details
  4. Click "Create Task"

The task will automatically be scheduled for the selected day.

üí∞ Subscription & Billing

What's the difference between Free and Pro plans?

The Free plan includes:

  • Up to 10 tasks
  • Basic calendar
  • Email support

The Pro plan includes:

  • Unlimited tasks
  • Advanced calendar
  • Priority support
  • Team collaboration
How do I upgrade to the Pro plan?

To upgrade to the Pro plan:

  1. Go to the Subscriptions page
  2. Click "See available plans"
  3. Select the Pro plan
  4. Click "Upgrade Now"
  5. Enter your payment information
  6. Click "Subscribe"
How do I cancel my subscription?

To cancel your subscription:

  1. Go to the Subscriptions page
  2. Click "Unsubscribe now"
  3. Confirm your cancellation in the popup dialog

Your subscription will remain active until the end of your current billing period.

Will I lose access to premium features if I cancel?

No, you will continue to have access to premium features until the end of your current billing period. After that, your account will revert to the Free plan, and you'll only have access to the features included in that plan.
